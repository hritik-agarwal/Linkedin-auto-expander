Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Auto Expander

Last updated: September 2025

Overview

LinkedIn Auto Expander is a Chrome extension that automatically expands truncated LinkedIn posts and comments. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our extension.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely locally on your device and does not communicate with any external servers.

Permissions Used

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to access the currently active LinkedIn tab to detect and expand truncated content.
  • storage: Used to remember your preference for enabling/disabling the extension. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • linkedin.com access: Required to function on LinkedIn pages where the extension operates.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage to save your preference for enabling or disabling the auto-expand functionality. This setting is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. It does not communicate with any external servers or services.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there are no data security concerns. All functionality occurs locally within your browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing or GitHub repository.

Compliance

This extension complies with Chrome Web Store policies and does not violate any privacy regulations as it does not collect, process, or store any personal information.
